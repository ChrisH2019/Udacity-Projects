Greektown

Home to both varied restaurants and stores as well as a cultural museum and an annual parade, Greektown has managed to cultivate itself as a nightlife and retail district while remaining essentially Greek, making it an excellent place to visit.

Greektown is a dining and nightlife district located on the Near West Side of Chicago. It is one of many neighborhoods that make up the Near West Side and is a popular destination amongst tourists and Chicago residents alike. A place where old World traditions remain prevalent in the many neighborhood establishments.

Marking the continuation of Greek heritage in Chicago, visitors can explore The National Hellenic Museum, ride the Greektown Trolly, experience the annual Greek Independence Day parade, and feast during the Taste of Greece. Chicago's Greektown is the best sampling of Greek heritage outside of Athens.
